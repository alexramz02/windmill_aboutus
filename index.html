Who We Are:

Windmill Home Solutions is a real estate solutions company based out of Cheyenne.

We’re a family-owned business and focus on helping homeowners like you find solutions for your problem whether you’re going through a foreclosure, can’t sell your property, or just need to sell their house for all kinds of reasons.

What We Do:

We provide win-win solutions to help homeowners get out of their sticky situations… like Foreclosure, owning a burdensome property, probate, or anything else.

At Windmill Home Solutions, we focus on providing you with a solution to your situation so you can continue to do the things you love.

Our People

Rob and Crystal


We are Rob and Crystal Geringer – partners in life and in business. Married over 26 years, we have a raised a beautiful family with 3 amazing kids. We love spending time in the mountains in the summer and winter with friends and family and playing with our 3 labs.


Rob grew up helping his dad on the farm, and learning the importance of hard work. This work ethic and desire to work for himself is what pushed him to be an Entrepreneur. Crystal grew up in her family’s business, working with children. She has always had a passion for helping people.


Rob was trained as a civil engineer but has been involved in real estate in various forms since 2006. Following a career helping troubled youth and their families, Crystal joined our real estate business full-time in 2017 and loves to continue to help people. We love working for ourselves and working together as a couple. Rob’s experience in real estate coupled with Crystal’s passion for helping people sets us apart!

Mike

I look forward to facing life head on and jumping the inevitable hurdles it will throw my way. There’s not much on this Earth more satisfying than conquering your daily, weekly, and yearly goals and rejoicing in your victories with the ones you love.
